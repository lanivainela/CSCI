It's up to us

You've learnt what Climate change is, the impact it has on our people and environment, countries that are being affected by Climate change, and lastly some inspiring messages from the poem "Rise" Now, it all comes down to how we all can do our part in helping to fight against Climate change.

How can we all do our part?

There are many ways we can all do our part and here's how you can help:

  • Save energy at home such as using less energy by lowering your heating and cooling use, switching to LED light bulbs, and washing your laundry with cold water.
  • Changing your homes source of energy such as switching to renewable sources including wind or solar which can help reduce your carbon footprint.
  • Having a different way of transport such as walking or biking
  • Reducing, Reusing, and Recycling to ensure that our environment is clean and well looked after.
