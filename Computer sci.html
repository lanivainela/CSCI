Global Action Against Climate Change

Working together for a greener planet!

1. What is Climate Change?

Climate change is define as long terms shifts in the earth's temperature and weather patterns. These changes impact on our environment, people and animals all across the world.

2. Impacts on People and Future Generations

Rising sea levels, droughts, and heatwaves are already affecting communities worldwide. Future generations will inherit a planet with limited resources, damaged ecosystems, and more frequent climate-related disasters unless we act now.

3. Countries Being Affected

Greenland is experiencing rapid ice melt, contributing to sea level rise and altering ocean currents. The Marshall Islands face rising tides that threaten homes, culture, and entire communities, making climate migration a growing reality.

5. Global Action & How You Can Help

  • Reduce waste, recycle, and limit plastic use.
  • Support clean energy and reduce fossil fuel use.
  • Raise awareness through art, social media, and community efforts.
  • Support policies and leaders focused on sustainability.

Together, we can protect the Earth for ourselves and future generations.
